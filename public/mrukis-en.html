03 / Monika & Raimondas

A farm that became a way of life.

MR. ŪKIS is a name coded from our initials — and a promise to offer only what we want to see on our own table.

Returning to our families' farming traditions, we chose French-origin Limousin cattle for our homestead near Kretinga. They thrive in our climate, while slow growth gives the beef its remarkable texture and flavour.

  • 100% raised on our farm
  • 48 h from butchery to packing
  • LT nationwide delivery
