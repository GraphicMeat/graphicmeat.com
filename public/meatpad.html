No invisible service

MeatPad does not upload code to provide editor intelligence.

It detects language servers already installed on your Mac and launches them inside the project. If a server is absent, ordinary editing and local completion keep working.

The Mac App Store sandbox cannot execute user-installed language servers. Full LSP support therefore belongs to the notarized direct-download build.
