Everything worth knowing about our farm, beef, ordering and delivery.

Who is MR. Ūkis?

We are Monika and Raimondas, founders and custodians of MR. Ūkis. The farm is our way of life, built around respect for nature, animals and people.

What makes the farm different?

Our cattle graze freely on natural meadows and receive natural feed without hormones or antibiotics. We also breed Limousin cattle and offer more than twenty different cuts.

What is special about Limousin beef?

Limousin cattle have fine bones and high muscling. Their beef is naturally lean, rich in protein, nutritious and flavourful.

How can I order?

Order through our online shop or contact the official MR. Ūkis Facebook account.

Where do you deliver?

We deliver throughout Lithuania. We deliver in the Klaipėda region ourselves; elsewhere, temperature-controlled delivery is provided by DPD Fresh.

How can I pay?

Pay cash on delivery or collection, or by bank transfer after confirmation. Because pack weights vary, the final amount is confirmed after weighing and packing.

How do I cancel an order?

Notify us immediately using the order contact shown in your official confirmation.

How much is delivery?
  • Klaipėda region: €6 below €60; free from €60.
  • Nationwide with DPD Fresh: €10 below €60; €6 from €60 to €119.99; free from €120.
  • These terms exclude the Curonian Spit.

Delivery prices may change. The final charge is confirmed during ordering.
