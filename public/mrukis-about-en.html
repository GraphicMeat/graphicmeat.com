01 / Our story

From pasture
to table.

We work hard to control every process involved in bringing quality beef to your table.

Our journey began when we settled on a homestead near Kretinga and returned to our agricultural roots. With deep farming traditions in both families, we wanted to build something true to our standards and bring natural meat production back into everyday life.

We breed and raise Limousin cattle ourselves, prepare their feed from the first blade of grass and care for every part of the process.
