PhotoBooks

Beautiful photobooks, automatically.

PhotoBooks is a native app for iPhone, iPad and Mac that turns your photo library into a print-ready album. Pull in photos from Apple Photos or your files, let the layout engine arrange them into clean album pages, tweak anything you like, then export a PDF ready for print or sharing.

// What it does

  • Smart auto-layout

    A hybrid engine (templates + generative page partitioning, unified by a scorer) arranges your photos into balanced spreads.

  • Import from Apple Photos

    Bring photos straight from your Apple Photos library or from files on disk — no manual wrangling.

  • Print-ready PDF export

    Export a PDF built for Blurb PDF-to-Book or generic print services — sized and bled correctly.

  • Shareable digital PDF

    Prefer screens? Export a lightweight digital PDF to share your album with anyone.

  • WYSIWYG editing

    What you see on screen is what prints — the same layout math drives both preview and PDF.

  • Native on iOS & macOS

    Built in SwiftUI for iOS 18+ and macOS 15+ — a real Apple-ecosystem app, not a web wrapper.

// How it works

  1. Import

    Choose photos from Apple Photos or the filesystem.

  2. Auto-arrange

    The layout engine builds album pages you can fine-tune.

  3. Export

    Save a print-ready PDF or a shareable digital copy.
