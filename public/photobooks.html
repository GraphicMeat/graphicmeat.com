// Why you'll want it

  • A whole book in seconds

    The layout engine (templates + generative page partitioning, unified by a scorer) fills every spread for you — no dragging photos into boxes one by one.

  • Your photos stay on your Mac

    Everything runs on-device. No upload, no cloud account, no one else's servers touching your memories.

  • Free — just download it

    No sign-up, no trial timer, no watermark on your export. Grab the .dmg and open it.

  • Print-ready PDF export

    Export sized and bled correctly for Blurb PDF-to-Book or any generic print service — send it straight off.

  • What you see is what prints

    The same layout math drives the screen preview and the PDF, so there are no surprises on paper.

  • Native, and it stays current

    Built in SwiftUI for macOS — not a web wrapper — and it updates itself in the background as new versions ship.

// Three steps to a book

  1. Import

    Pull photos from Apple Photos or pick files on disk.

  2. Auto-arrange

    The engine builds album pages you can fine-tune.

  3. Export

    Save a print-ready PDF or a shareable digital copy.
