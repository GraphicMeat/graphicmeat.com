General provisions

The data controllers are farmers Raimondas Pareigis, individual activity certificate No. 610486, and Monika Pareigienė, at Genčų Medsėdžiai, Kretinga, LT 97251.

This policy covers data provided through the website, online shop, customer account, social media and direct communication. Data is handled under the GDPR and Lithuanian law.

Data we handle

We receive data when you register, order goods or services, or contact us. The site may automatically collect IP address, cookie information, browsing history and preferences. Third parties may provide delivery details only with an appropriate lawful basis.

The farm territory and events may be recorded for security purposes.

How we use data

Data is used to fulfil orders, administer contracts, send essential service messages, protect legitimate interests and meet legal duties. We collect only what is necessary and handle it lawfully, fairly and transparently.

Who receives data?

Data may be shared with accounting, delivery, payment, marketing, legal or audit partners only where necessary. It may also be disclosed to public authorities, law enforcement and courts where required by law.

Your rights

You may request information, access, correction, completion, deletion or restriction of your data, and withdraw consent. You may object to direct marketing at any time without giving a reason.

Cookies

Cookies support site functionality and improvement. They may process IP address, visited areas and time spent on the site. You can delete or block cookies in your browser, although some functions may then be limited.
